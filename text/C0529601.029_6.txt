Respondent under Case Number 2015-0771B, alleging, inter alia, that

the Respondent, while working in her capacity as a physician

for Percocet 5/APAP 325 mg (an opioid medication and Schedule II
controlled dangerous substance), without their authorization,
permission or consent, and filled the prescriptions at area
pharmacies. The Respondent wrote the prescriptions, which were
typically for 60 pills each, at approximately two-week intervals, from
October 2014, to April 2015. The Respondent was not a patient or
under the care of any of the providers in whose names she wrote
the prescriptions.
