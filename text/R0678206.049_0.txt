ORDER AFTER SHOW CAUSE HEARING

On September 19, 2018, Farison Padavil, Radiographer, entered into a Consent Order
with Board Disciplinary Panel A (“Panel A”) of the Maryland State Board of Physicians
(“Board”) wherein he was reprimanded, ordered to pay a fine, and ordered to contact the
Maryland Professional Rehabilitation Program (“MPRP”) to schedule an initial evaluation and
possible enrollment. On February 22, 2019, Panel A issued a Violation of Consent Order and
Notice to Show Cause, charging Mr. Padavil with violating the terms and conditions of the
September 19, 2018 Consent Order by failing to sign the required release forms necessary for
enrollment and participation in MPRP. On May 8, 2019, Panel A held a show cause hearing
where Mr. Padavil had the opportunity to argue why his license should not be subject to farther
discipline.
