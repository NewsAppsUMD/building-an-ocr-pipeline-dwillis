Rehabilitation Program:

(a) The Respondent shall fully and timely cooperate and comply
with all MPRP’s referrals, rules, and requirements, including,
but not limited to, the terms and conditions of the Participant
Rehabilitation Agreement(s) and Participant Rehabilitation

comply with all therapy, treatment, evaluations, and screenings

release/consent forms requested by the Board and MPRP,

files in a public order. The Respondent shall not withdraw his
release/consent;

forms to authorize MPRP to exchange with (i.e., disclose to

records of the Respondent, including, but not limited to, mental
health and drug or alcohol evaluation and treatment records.
