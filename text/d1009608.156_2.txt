Except as specified in Health Occupations Article, § 14-302, Annotated Code of
Maryland,’ an individual shall be a licensed Maryland physician in order to
practice telemedicine if one or both of the following occurs:
A. The individual practicing telemedicine is physically located in Maryland;
B. The patient is in Maryland.
Subsequent to the expiration of all licenses to practice medicine in any state and
while residing in Maryland, the Respondent provided psychotherapy by Skype? to
a patient (“Patient A”) who resides in Colorado.
Subsequent to the expiration of all licenses to practice medicine in any state and
while residing the Maryland, the Respondent prescribed medication to a patient
(“Patient B”) who resides in Colorado.
