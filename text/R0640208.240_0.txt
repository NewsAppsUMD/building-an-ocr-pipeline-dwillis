PROCEDURAL HISTORY

State of Maryland, on June 21, 2002, under license number RO6402. On January 17,2018, in the
District Court of Maryland for Garrett County, Case Number 3X00025259, Ms. Ashby entered
an Alford Plea to, and was convicted of, one count of Theft-Scheme: $10,000 to $100,000, in
violation of Md. Code Ann., Criminal Law Article § 7-104. The Court sentenced Ms. Ashby to
eighteen months of incarceration, with all eighteen months suspended, and three years of
supervised probation with conditions that included payment of restitution to her former employer
in the amount of $34,433.90,

Petition”) pursuant to Md. Code Ann., Health Occupations Article, § 14-5B-14(c)(2). The statute

provides:
