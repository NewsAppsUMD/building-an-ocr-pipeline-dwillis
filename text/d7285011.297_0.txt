ORDER FOR SUMMARY SUSPENSION OF LICENSE TO PRACTICE MEDICINE

Disciplinary Panel B of the Maryland State Board of Physicians (the “Board”)
hereby SUMMARILY SUSPENDS the medical license of PREETI ROUT, M.D. (the
“Respondent’), License Number D72850. Disciplinary Panel B takes such action
pursuant to its authority under the Administrative Procedure Act (the “APA”), Md. Code
Ann., State Gov't § 10-226(c)(2)(i) (2014 Repl. Vol. & 2015 Supp.), concluding that the
public health, safety, or welfare imperatively requires emergency action.

On November 29, 2017, Disciplinary Panel B held a pre-deprivation hearing for
the Respondent to show cause why the Order for Summary Suspension of License to
Practice Medicine should not be issued.
