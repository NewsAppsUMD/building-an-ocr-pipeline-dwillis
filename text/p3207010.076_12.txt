NOTICE OF POSSIBLE SANCTIONS

 

If, after a hearing, Disciplinary Panel A of the Board finds that there are grounds
for action under COMAR 10.32.07.07, it may impose disciplinary sanctions against the
Respondent's registration, including reprimand, probation, suspension, revocation, or
other action including, but not limited to, limiting the privilege to practice, requiring
further education, or admonishment. .
