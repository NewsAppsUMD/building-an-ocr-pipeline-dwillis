The Complaint

6. On or about April 12, 2022, the Board received a Mandated 10-Day Report
(the “Report”) from the Facility. According to the Report, the Respondent exhibited
indicators of impairment and/or intoxication while working at the Facility on April 5,
2022.

7. The Report stated that the Respondent acknowledged being under the
influence of alcohol while at work at the Facility on April 5, 2022. The Facility
terminated his employment effective April 7, 2022.

Board Investigation

8. The Board initiated an investigation based on the Report from the Facility.
The Board notified the Respondent of its investigation by letter dated April 25, 2022 and
directed him to provide a written response.

9. As a part of the investigation, Board staff interviewed the Respondent and

several of his colleagues.

Respondent’s Written Response

10. By letter dated May 9, 2022, the Respondent provided a written response in
which he took “full responsibility for [his] conduct on April 5, 2022.”

11. The Respondent stated “on April 4, 2022, I consumed wine in the evening.
I went to sleep but woke up at approximately 1:00 a.m. or 2:00 a.m. with anxiety and
