June 13, 2022 Consent Order

As a resolution to the charges, Ms. Chastain entered into a Consent Order with Panel B, on
June 13, 2022, wherein the Panel found that Ms. Chastain violated the disciplinary grounds, as
alleged in the charges. Pursuant to the Consent Order, the summary suspension was terminated,
Ms. Chastain was reprimanded, and her license to practice radiography was suspended for a
minimum of six months, with the requirement that she enroll in MPRP.
