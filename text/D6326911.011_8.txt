Respondent: was guilty of unprofessional conduct in the practice of medicine, see Health Occ. §

narcotic or CDS that is in excess of therapeutic amounts or without valid medical indication, see

treatment and has been abstinent for six months.

that the Respondent’s actions do not pose a substantial likelihood of risk of serious harm to the
