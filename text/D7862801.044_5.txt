CONCLUSIONS OF LAW

Based upon the findings of fact, Panel B concludes that the Respondent: is guilty of
unprofessional conduct in the practice of medicine, in violation of Health Occ. § 14-
404(a)(3)(ii); was habitually intoxicated, in violation of Health Occ. § 14-404(a)(7); and
provided professional services under the influence of alcohol, in violation of Health Occ.
§ 14-404(a)(9)G).
