CONCLUSIONS OF LAW
Based on the above findings of fact, Panel B concludes that the Respondent: is guilty of
unprofessional conduct in the practice of athletic training, in violation of Health Occ. § 14-5D-
14(a)(3); is professionally, physically, or mentally incompetent, in violation of Health Occ. § 14-
5D-14(a)(4); was habitually intoxicated, in violation of Health Occ. § 14-5D-14(a)(6); and
provided professional services while under the influence of alcohol, in violation of Health Occ. §
14-5D-14(a)(8)Q).
