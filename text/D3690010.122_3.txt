2020 Final Decision and Order

9. On August 31, 2020, Disciplinary Panel A of the Board issued disciplinary
charges against the Respondent based on a complaint from an investigator at the College.
The complaint stated that a female physician assistant reported to the College that the
Respondent made sexually inappropriate statements to her while she was participating in

a clinical rotation at the Respondent’s office as part of her studies.

10. The Respondent requested a hearing on the charges before the Office of
Administrative Hearings, after which Panel B issued a Final Decision and Order on
February 24, 2022. In its Final Decision and Order, Panel B concluded that the
Respondent was guilty of unprofessional conduct in the practice of medicine, in violation

of Health Occ. § 14-404(a)(3)(ai).

11. Pursuant to the Final Decision and Order, Panel B reprimanded the
Respondent and placed him on probation for a minimum of two (2) years, subject to
terms and conditions including enrollment in MPRP and fully and timely complying with
its referrals, rules, and requirements, including, but not limited to, full participation and

compliance with all screening, evaluations, therapy and treatment as directed.
