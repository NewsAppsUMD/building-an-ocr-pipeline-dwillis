Introduction

1; Panel B is summarily suspending the Respondent’s license to practice
medicine after a Board investigation revealed that he has a health condition that affects his
ability to practice medicine safely.

Background/Licensing Information

2. The Respondent was originally issued a license to practice medicine in
Maryland on May 21, 2013, under License Number D76104. The Respondent has retained
continuous licensure since that time. The Respondent’s license expires on September 30,
2024, subject to renewal.

3. The Respondent is board-certified in anesthesiology.

4. At all relevant times, the Respondent practiced at a hospital? (the “Hospital”)
in Maryland until resigning, on or about February 24, 2023, while the Respondent was
under investigation.

The Report

5. The Board initiated an investigation of the Respondent after receiving a
Mandated 10-Day Report (the “Report”) from the Hospital on December 20, 2022. The
Report stated that the Respondent “was placed on precautionary suspension pending further

information regarding potential impairment.”
