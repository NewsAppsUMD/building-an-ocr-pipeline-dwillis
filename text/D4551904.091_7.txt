(Board) issued charges against Julian P, Choe, M.D. (Respondent) alleging violations of the
State law governing the practice of medicine. Md. Code Ann., Health Occ. §§ 14-101 through
14-508, and 14-601 through 14-607 (2014 & Supp. 2020). Specifically, the Respondent is

misconduct regulations under the Code of Maryland Regulations (COMAR) 10.32.17. The
disciplinary panel to which the complaint was assigned held a meeting with the Respondent on
