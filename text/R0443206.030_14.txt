CONCLUSIONS OF LAW

Based upon the findings of fact, Panel A concludes that the Respondent:
fraudulently or deceptively obtained or attempted to obtain a license, in violation of Health
Occ. § 14-5B-14(a)(1); is guilty of immoral and unprofessional conduct in the practice of
radiography, in violation of Health Occ. § 14-5B-14(a)(3); willfully made or filed a false
report in the practice of radiography, in violation of Health Occ. § 14-5B-14(a)(10); and
engaged in sexual misconduct, in violation of COMAR 10.32.17.03, see Health Occ. § 1-
212.
