Based upon the findings of fact, Panel B concludes that the Respondent: failed to meet
appropriate standards as determined by appropriate peer review for the delivery of quality medical
and surgical care performed in an outpatient surgical facility, office, hospital, or any other location

records as determined by appropriate peer review, in violation of Health Occ. § 14-404(a)(40).
