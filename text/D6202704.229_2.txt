Respondent has failed to comply with any term or condition imposed by the Consent Order, the
disciplinary panel may reprimand the Respondent, place the Respondent on an extended period
of probation, or suspend or revoke the Respondent’s license to practice medicine in Maryland.
The disciplinary panel may, in addition to one or more of the sanctions set forth above, impose a
civil monetary fine on the Respondent; and it is further

and conditions of this Order; and it is further
