the effective date of this Order. The written request should be made to: Christine A.
Farrelly, Executive Director, Maryland State Board of Physicians, 4201 Patterson
Avenue, Baltimore, Maryland 21215, with a copy mailed to: Victoria H. Pepper,
Assistant Attorney General, Health Occupations Prosecution and Litigation Unit, 300
West Preston Street, Suite 201, Baltimore, Maryland 21210.The Respondent may also
request a hearing. The applicable procedures are set forth in Md Code Regs.
