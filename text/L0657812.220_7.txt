SANCTION

The ALJ proposed that the Respondent’s license to practice respiratory care in Maryland
be revoked. No exceptions to the ALJ’s proposed default order were filed. The Panel finds that
revocation is warranted.

ORDER

Based upon the findings of fact and conclusions of law, it is, on the affirmative vote of a
majority of the quorum of Board Disciplinary Panel A, hereby

ORDERED that the license of Liuchuan Chen, R.C.P to practice respiratory care in
Maryland is REVOKED; and it is further
