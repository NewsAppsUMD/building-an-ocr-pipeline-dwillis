NOTICE OF POSSIBLE SANCTIONS
If, after a hearing, a Board disciplinary panel finds that there are grounds for

action under Health Occ. §§ 14-S5F-14, 14-5F-29(a), 14-5F-30(a)(1) and/or Health Occ. §
15-314(a)(3)(ii), a Board disciplinary panel may impose disciplinary sanctions against the
Respondent’s license. With regard to Health Occ. § 14-S5F-29(a) and/or Health Occ. §
14-5F-30(a)(1), a Board disciplinary panel may impose a civil fine of not more than
$50,000. With regard to Health Occ. § 15-314(a)(ii), a disciplinary panel may impose
disciplinary sanctions including revocation, suspension, reprimand, and/or probation, and
may impose a fine.
