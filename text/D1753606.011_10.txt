ORDER

On an affirmative vote of a majority of a quorum of Disciplinary Panel B, it is hereby

ORDERED that the license of Akram Salihi, M.D. to practice medicine in Maryland,
license number D17536, is REVOKED; and it is further

ORDERED that the July 15, 2020, order imposing a summary suspension upon Dr.
Salihi’s medical license is terminated as moot; and it is further

ORDERED that, if Dr. Salihi regains competence, Dr. Salihi may apply for reinstatement
of his license to practice medicine after a minimum of one (1) year from the date of this Order;

and it is further

ORDERED that this is a public document. See Health Occ. §§ 1-607, 14-411.1(b)(2) and
Gen. Prov. § 4-333(b)(6).
