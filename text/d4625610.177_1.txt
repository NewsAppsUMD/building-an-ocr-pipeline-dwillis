 

FINDINGS OF FACT

Disciplinary Panel A finds:

1.

At all times relevant, the Respondent was and is licensed to practice medicine in
Maryland. The Respondent was initially licensed to practice medicine in
Maryland on June 22, 1994. His license is scheduled to expire on September 30,
2018.

The Respondent is board-certified in anesthesiology.

From October 2016 to June 2017, the Respondent was employed at a federal
facility (the “Facility’) in a state other than Maryland.

In June 2017, the Facility terminated the Respondent for diverting for his own use
a quantity of a Controlled Dangerous Substance. The Respondent obtained the
drug by misrepresentation. Specifically, the Respondent falsely entered patient
information and a specific quantity of the drug to be used in a medical procedure.
The Respondent has been indicted in federal court on several counts of obtaining
the drug by misrepresentation, fraud, deception and subterfuge.’

CONCLUSIONS OF LAW
