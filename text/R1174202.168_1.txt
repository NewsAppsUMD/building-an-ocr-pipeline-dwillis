In this section. “Central Repository” means the Criminal Justice
Information System Central Repository of the Department of Public

Safety and Correctional Services.

An applicant, a licensee, or a certificate holder shall apply to the
Central Repository for a State and national criminal history records
check.

As part of the application required under subsection (b) of this

section, an individual shall submit to the Central Repository:

(1)

Two complete sets of legible fingerprints taken on
forms approved by the Director of the Central
Repository and the Director of the Federal Bureau of
Investigation:

The fee authorized under § 710-221(b)(7) of the
Criminal Procedure Article for access to State criminal
history records; and

The mandatory processing fee required by the
Federal Bureau of Investigation for a national criminal
history records check.
