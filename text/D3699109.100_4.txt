12, 2018, because of “reasonable grounds to believe that the health or safety of any
patient or staff may be in imminent danger as a result of your continued provision
of services.”

The Board initiated an investigation that included subpoenaing the Respondent’s
Hospital personnel file and interviewing the chair of the Respondent’s department
(“Physician 1”),

Review of the Respondent’s personnel file revealed that in August 2018 concerns
were raised regarding the Respondent’s ability to perform gynecologic surgeries in
a safe manner,

Specifically, on August 16, 2018, the Respondent began performing a pelviscopy

and total laparascopic hysterectomy on a patient.
