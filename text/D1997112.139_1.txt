 

 

At all times relevant hereto, the Respondent was licensed to practice
medicine in Maryland. The Respondent was initially licensed to practice
medicine in Maryland on November 18, 1976. His license is scheduled to
expire on September 30, 2021.

The Respondent practices Internal Medicine and Gastroenterology in a
private office.

The Respondent was awarded privileges to practice at a hospital in
Maryland, hereinafter, “Hospital AZ”

On or about August 8, 2019, the Board received a notification from
Hospital A that the Respondent’s privileges to practice as a physician at
Hospital A were suspended due to concerns about his ability to practice
safely.

Hospital A referred the Respondent to a Physician Health Program (the
“Program”), which directed the Respondent to undergo an appropriate
evaluation.

On or about October 30, 2019, the Respondent participated in a
comprehensive evaluation conducted by qualified, licensed practitioners,
who issued a subsequent report on November 4, 2019 with specific

findings.
