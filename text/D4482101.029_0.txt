FINAL ORDER OF REVOCATION OF MEDICAL LICENSE

 

On November 16, 2018 Disciplinary Panel A (“Panel A”) of the Maryland State
Board of Physicians (the “Board”) notified DAVID MORTON SILVERSTEIN, M.D. (the
“Respondent”), License Number D44821, of its INTENT TO REVOKE the
Respondent’s license to practice medicine in the State of Maryland. The Notice informed
the Respondent that unless the Respondent requested a hearing in writing within 30 days
of the date of mailing of the Notice, Panel A intended to sign this Final Order, which was
enclosed. More than 30 days have elapsed, and the Respondent failed to request a hearing.
Therefore, Panel A hereby revokes the Respondent’s license to practice medicine.

The basis for Panel A’s action was pursuant to the Maryland Medical Practice Act
(the “Act”), Md. Code Ann., Health Occ. ("Health Occ.) §§ 14-401 ef seg. (2014 Repl.
Vol. and 2017 Supp.). and COMAR 10.32.02. 14(A).
