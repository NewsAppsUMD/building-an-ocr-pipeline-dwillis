BACKGROUND
On or about December 10, 2018, the Maryland State Board of Physicians (the “Board”)
received a written request from the Baltimore City Office of the Child Support Enforcement
Administration of the Maryland Department of Human Resources (the “Administration’”) to
suspend the physician’s assistant license held by Fahim Nadir Kashif, PA-C, (the “Respondent”),
License No. C03457, for delinquent child support. This action was to be taken by authority of
Md. Code Ann., Fam. Law § 10-119.3 (2012 Repl. Vol.), which provides, in pertinent part:
