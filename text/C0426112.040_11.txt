NOTICE OF POSSIBLE SANCTIONS

 

If, after a hearing, the Board or a disciplinary panel of the Board finds that there
are grounds for action under the above-cited provisions of law and/or terms of the
Consent Order, the Board or disciplinary panel may reprimand the Respondent, place the
Respondent on probation with appropriate terms and conditions, or suspend or revoke the
Respondent’s license to practice as a physician assistant in Maryland. The Board or
disciplinary panel may, in addition to one or more of the sanctions set forth above,

impose a civil monetary fine upon the Respondent.
