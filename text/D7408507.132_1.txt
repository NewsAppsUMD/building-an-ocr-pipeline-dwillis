Introduction

1. Panel B is summarily suspending the Respondent’s license to practice
medicine after a Board investigation determined that he has a health condition that affects
his ability to practice medicine safely.

Background/Licensing Information

2. The Respondent was originally issued a license to practice medicine in
Maryland on May 1, 2012, under License Number D74085. The Respondent has not
retained continuous licensure in Maryland. The Respondent allowed his license to lapse
on or about September 30, 2016. He was later reinstated on September 7, 2021. He is also
licensed in the District of Columbia.

3. The Respondent is Board Certified in Anesthesiology, and Anesthesiology,
Critical Care Medicine. He has hospital privileges at a hospital in Baltimore County,
Maryland.

4. At all times relevant hereto, the Respondent practiced medicine at a health
care facility (the “Facility”)* that has several locations in Maryland and the District of
Columbia.

5: The Respondent began working at the Facility on February 14, 2022, and

was terminated on April 7, 2022.

* For confidentiality reasons, the name of the health care facility will not be identified in this document. The
respondent is aware of the identity of the health care facility.
