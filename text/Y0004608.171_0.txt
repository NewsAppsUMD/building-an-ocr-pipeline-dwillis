ORDER SUSPENDING LICENSE TO PRACTICE
PERFUSION IN THE STATE OF MARYLAND

On June 17, 2021, Disciplinary Panel B (the “Panel”) of the Maryland State Board of Physicians
(the “Board”) and Stephen L. Robinson entered into a Consent Order, wherein Panel B concluded, as a
matter of law, that Mr. Robinson failed to submit to a criminal history records check (“CHRC’).!
Pursuant to the Consent Order, Mr. Robinson was reprimanded, ordered to complete the CHRC process
WITHIN SIXTY (60) DAYS from the effective date of the Consent Order (June 17, 2021), and ordered
to pay a civil fine of $500.00 within six (6) months of the Consent Order. The Consent Order further
ordered that if Mr. Robinson failed to complete the CHRC process and the Board did not receive the
completed CHRC report within 60 days from the effective date of the Consent Order, Mr. Robinson’s
license shall be automatically suspended from the 61% day until the Board receives Mr. Robinson’s
completed CHRC report.

Mr. Robinson has paid the $500.00 civil fine. The Board did not receive a completed CHRC
report. It is thus, pursuant to the June 17, 2021 Consent Order of Disciplinary Panel B, hereby

ORDERED that Mr. Robinson’s license to practice perfusion in the State of Maryland is hereby

SUSPENDED; and it is further
