FINAL DECISION AND ORDER
On July 9, 2020, Disciplinary Panel B of the Maryland State Board of Physicians (the
“Board’”) issued Charges under the Maryland Respiratory Care Practitioners Act (“Charges”)
against the Respondent Liuchuan Chen, R.C.P. The Charges alleged that the Respondent
violated the following grounds of the Health Occupations Article:
Health Occ. § 14-5A-17. Denials, suspensions, or revocation of licensee
(a) In general. -- Subject to the hearing provisions of § 14-405 of this title, a
disciplinary panel, on the affirmative vote of a majority of the quorum of
the disciplinary panel, may deny a license to any applicant, reprimand any
licensee, place any licensee on probation, or suspend or revoke a license,
if the applicant or licensee:
(1) Fraudulently or deceptively obtains or attempts to obtain a license

for the applicant or licensee or for another;
