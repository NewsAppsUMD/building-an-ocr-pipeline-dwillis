§ 14-607. Practicing without license.

Except as otherwise provided in this title,’ a person may not practice,
attempt to practice, or offer to practice medicine in this State unless
licensed by the Board.

Section 14-101 of the Act defines “practice medicine” as follows:

(o) Practice medicine.
(1) “Practice medicine” means to engage, with or without
compensation, in medical:
(i) Diagnosis:

(ii) Healing;
(iii) Treatment; or
(iv) Surgery.

(2) “Practice medicine” includes doing, undertaking,
professing to do, and attempting any of the following:
(i) Diagnosing, healing, treatment, preventing,
prescribing for, or removing any physical,
mental, or emotional ailment of an individual:

(2) By appliance, test, drug, operation, or
treatment{.]
