CHARGES UNDER THE MARYLAND NATUROPATHIC MEDICINE ACT
AND THE MARYLAND PHYSICIAN ASSISTANTS ACT

Disciplinary Panel B (“Panel B”) of the Maryland State Board of Physicians (the
“Board”) hereby charges RAELYNN M. TORZONE, PA-C (the “Respondent”),
License Number C06111, under the Maryland Naturopathic Medicine Act, Md. Code
Ann., Health Occ. (“Health Occ.”) §§ 14-SF-01 et seg. (2021 Repl. Vol.) and the
Maryland Physician Assistants Act, Health Occ. §§ 15-101 et seq. (2021 Repl. Vol.).

Panel B charges the Respondent under the following statutory grounds:

Health Occ. § 14-5F-29. Unlicensed practice prohibited.
(a) In general. — Except as otherwise provided in this subtitle, an

individual may not practice, attempt to practice, or offer to practice
naturopathic medicine in this State without a license.

(c) Civil fine. — Any individual who violates subsection (a) of this
section or § 14-5F-30 of this subtitle is subject to a civil fine of not
more than $50,000 to be levied by a disciplinary panel.
