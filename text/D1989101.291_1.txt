THE COMPLAINT

4. On or about November 20, 2019 the Board received a complaint from a
physician who is associated with the Respondent (“Physician A”) alleging that the
Respondent “is no longer competent to care for patients.”

BOARD INVESTIGATION

5. On receipt of the Complaint, the Board initiated an investigation into the
allegation. As part of its investigation, Board staff conducted interviews and subpoenaed
records including the Respondent’s medical records and medical records of patients.

6. The Board’s investigation revealed that the Respondent was involved in an
automobile accident in July 2018.

return to the medical practice until November 2019. During the Respondent’s absence,
Physician A assumed the care of Respondent’s patients. While caring for the Respondent’s
