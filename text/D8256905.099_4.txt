8. The Respondent’s actions including failure to complete the CHRC, as

Occ. § 14-404(a)(1), Fraudulently or deceptively obtaining or attempting to

404(a)(3)(ii), Is guilty of: (ii) Unprofessional conduct in the practice of
medicine; Health Occ. § 14-404(a)(33), Fails to cooperate with a lawful
investigation conducted by the Board or a disciplinary panel; and Health Occ.
§ 14-404(a)(36), Willfully makes or files a false report or record in the

practice of medicine.

CONCLUSIONS OF LAW
Based on the foregoing Findings of Fact, Panel B concludes as a matter of law that

the Respondent violated Health Occ. §§ 14-404(a) (1), (311), (33), (36) and (42).
