CONCLUSIONS OF LAW

 

Based of the above findings of fact, Panel A concludes that the Respondent is guilty
of unprofessional or inimoral conduct in the practice of respiratory care, in violation of
Health Occ. § 14-5A-17(a)(3), Is addicted to or habitually abuses any narcotic or controlled
dangerous substance as defined in § 5-101 of the Criminal Law Article, in violation of
Health Oce. § 14-5A-17(a)(7); and provides professional services while using any narcotic
or controlled dangerous substance as defined in § 5-101 or the Criminal Law Article or any
other drug that is in excess of therapeutic amounts or without valid medical indication, in
violation of Health Occ. § 14-5A-17(a)(8)(ii).
