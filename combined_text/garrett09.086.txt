ORDER

On August 1, 2016, the Maryland State Board of Physicians (“Board”) and January
Garrett entered into a public Consent Order, wherein the Board found that Ms. Garrett

practiced medicine without a license, in violation of Maryland Code Annotated, Health

Occupations §14-601." Under the Consent Order, Ms. Garrett agreed to cease and desist from
the unlicensed practice of medicine and pay a fine of $1,000.00 within thirty (30) days of the
Consent Order. Ms. Garrett has paid the $1,000.00 fine. It is thus hereby

ORDERED that the condition of the Consent Order that Ms. Garrett pay a fine of
$1,000.00 has been SATISFIED; and it is further

ORDERED that Ms. Garrett shall continue to CEASE AND DESIST from the
unlicensed practice of medicine, including the performance of cosmetic medical procedures as
defined in COMAR 10.32.09.02B(5); and it is further

ORDERED that this is a public document.
