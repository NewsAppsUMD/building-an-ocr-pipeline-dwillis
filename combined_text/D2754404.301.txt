ORDER

On December 29, 2020, Disciplinary Panel A (“Panel”) issued an Order for
Summary Suspension, which summarily suspended the license of John A. Vitarello Jr.,
M.D. On January 14, 2021, the Summary Suspension was affirmed after a post-
deprivation hearing before Panel A. On April 29, 2021, Panel A accepted the permanent
surrender of Dr. Vitarello’s medical license. It is thus hereby:

ORDERED that the summary suspension issued on December 29, 2020 is
TERMINATED AS MOOT; and it is further

ORDERED that this Order is a PUBLIC DOCUMENT.
