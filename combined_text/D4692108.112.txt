ORDER FOR SUMMARY SUSPENSION
OF LICENSE TO PRACTICE MEDICINE

Disciplinary Panel B (“Panel B”) of the Maryland State Board of Physicians (the
“Board”) hereby SUMMARILY SUSPENDS the license of GEORGE A. OYLER,
M.D., (the “Respondent”), license number D46921 to practice medicine in the State of
Maryland.

Panel B takes such action pursuant to its authority under Md. Code Ann., State
Govt § 10-226(c)(2) (2021 Repl. Vol.), concluding that the public health, safety or
welfare imperatively requires emergency action.
